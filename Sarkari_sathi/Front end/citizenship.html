Official Information & Visual Guide

Legal Overview

Nepali citizenship is primarily governed by the Constitution of Nepal and the Nepal Citizenship Act, 2006, generally granted by descent if either parent is a Nepali citizen. It is essential for legal identity, voting, property rights, and accessing government services for individuals aged 16 or older. Citizenship can be acquired by descent, birth, naturalization, or honorary, while dual citizenship is not permitted.
