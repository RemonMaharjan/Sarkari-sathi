Passport (राहदानी)

E-Passport Guidance & Types

Legal Overview

Nepalese passports, now issued as secure E-passports by the Department of Passport (DoP) under the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, are biometric travel documents available to all Nepali citizens. Applications require online pre-enrollment via the official portal followed by in-person submission at local district offices (DAOs) or embassies.

Types of Passports in Nepal

There are 4 main types of passports issued by the Government of Nepal.

Ordinary (Chocolate Brown)

Official (Blue)

Diplomatic (Red)

Service (Orange)
