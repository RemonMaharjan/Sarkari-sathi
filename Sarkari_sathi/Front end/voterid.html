Voter ID (मतदाता परिचय पत्र)

Registration & Election Commission Guidance

Legal Overview

In Nepal, the Voter ID Card (Matadata Parichaya Patra) is an essential biometric identification document issued by the Election Commission of Nepal (ECN). It is mandatory for casting a vote in national and local elections.
